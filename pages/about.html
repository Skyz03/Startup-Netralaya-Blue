Meet Our Professional Team Members

Prof. Dr. Rohit Saiju, MD

Dr. Rohit Saiju is a consultant eye surgeon who specializes in treating cataracts, eyelid, orbit, and tears draining system diseases. He completed his MD in Ophthalmology from Tribhuvan University in Kathmandu in 2001 and a one-year clinical fellowship in Orbit, Plastic, and Lacrimal disorders at the Royal Victorian Eye and Ear Hospital in Melbourne in 2004. Dr. Saiju is a senior visiting consultant at the Tilganga Institute of Ophthalmology and is a prominent cataract and plastic eye surgeon. He has received several awards, including the APAO Distinguished Service Award (Tokyo) 2014, the APAO Achievement Award (China) 2021, and the SAO Excellence Award (Delhi) 2022. He is currently a Professor of Ophthalmology at the National Academy of Medical Sciences and a council member of the APAO.

Prof. Dr. Govinda Poudyal, MD

Prof. Dr. Govinda Poudyal is a renowned cataract and Retinal Surgeon in the country, working in Tilganga Institute of Ophthalmologyfor 25 years. He is a Professor and academic director in Tilganga Institute of Ophthalmology. He completed his MD in Ophthalmology from Institute of Medicine, Tribhuvan University, Nepal in 1996. He pursued a highly prestigious postgraduate one year clinical fellowship in vitero-retina at Royal Victorian Eye & Ear Hospital, Australia in 2000. He has been involved in dozens of hospital based and community based researches in the field of Cataract and Retina for last 2 decades. He has received Distinguished Service Award from Asia-Pacific Academy of Ophthalmology (APAO).

Dr. Sanyam Bajimaya, MD

Dr. Bajimaya received his medical degree (MBBS-2001) from Chittagong Medical College, Bangladesh and MD in Ophthalmology from BPKLCOS, IOM Tribhuvan University Kathmandu He joined the Lumbini Eye Institute (LEI), Bhairahawa in 2007 and had done his Advanced Phaco training under leadership of Prof. Junsuke Akura, AOCA. He joined Tilganga Institute of Ophthalmology in 2010 and completed his Vitreo-Retina fellowship from John A. Moran Eye Center University of Utah, USA in 2012. His special interests include Cataracts, Intra-ocular lens (IOL) management, Vitreo-Retina specialty and Pediatric Retina including Retinopathy of Prematurity. Dr. Bajimaya had elected at the post of Council Member of APVRS (2017-19) and Secretary-General of Nepal Vitreo-Retina Society (NVRS 2017-22). He has International Memberships from the American Academy of Ophthalmology (AAO).

Dr. Pragati Gautam Adhikari, MD

Dr. Pragati is a consultant glaucoma specialist working as an Assistant Professor in B.P. Koirala Lions center for ophthalmic studies, Maharajgunj Medical Campus, TU since 2011AD. She has been working as a comprehensive ophthalmologist with special interest in glaucoma. She looks about 100 general cases per week, 20 to 30 glaucoma cases per week. She has completed a short course on Glaucoma Diagnosis and Management Training from Tilganga Institute of Ophthalmology and 3 month training in Medical, Laser and Surgical management of Glaucoma from Lumbini Eye Institute.

Dr. Diwa Hamal Lamichhane

Dr. Diwa Hamal Lamichhane is a kind and energetic young ophthalmologist practicing in this field since completion of MD Ophthalmology from Manipal in 2012. From 2013- 2021 her contribution as anterior segment and Oculoplastic surgeon in Biratnagar Eye hospital has well acknowledged. She has done Anterior segment fellowship from BEH in 2014 and Orbit and Oculoplasty fellowship from Tilganga Institute of Ophthalmology in 2017.She has a record of over 18,000 cataract and hundred od different Oculoplastic surgeries with special interest in Aesthetic and Endoscopic DCR surgery. She is well known for her individual care to the patients in customized way to satisfy them.
